In December 2019, an outbreak of pneumonia occured near the Huanan Seafood Market in Wuhan, China.

By the end of January, over 57 million people were quarantined in 16 cities throughout Hubei province.

Assisted by the Chinese new year migration, the virus initially spread to other Chinese provinces.

The virus has not spread as rapidly in other provinces.

The virus was soon carried to other countries by international travellers.

The spread of the virus in other countries has been minimized due to quarantine and travel restrictions.

Thankfully, recoveries have outpaced deaths.
